Getting Started

To get the most of your FrontlineCloud workspace, you’ll need to complete each of the steps below. As you click on each step, a tray will slide out from the right side of the screen for you to setup and configure Frontline. Complete Steps 1-3 to get started. If you ever need any help, click the ‘Get Support’ link at the bottom of this page.

  1. Connect to a Mobile Network
    • Connect to a web aggregator or an Android device via FrontlineSync. CLICK HERE to begin configuring your account.
    • For more information about FrontlineSync vs Web Aggregators, check out this video. (Link to YouTube video)
  2. Configure your Radio Activities
    • As part of your FrontlineCloud powered Radio workspace, we’ve included a few activities to help you run more powerful engagements with your listeners. To start configuring your Activities, CLICK HERE. The Activities at the top of the tray are customized for Radio users.
  3. Send a Test Message
    • After you’ve configured your Activities and connected to a mobile network, its time to send a test message. Enter a contact number in the multi selector on the toolbar to the right. Enter some sample text to send to a user, and select which connection in the drop down below. Click send and wait for the magic!
  4. Upload Contacts (optional)
    • You can also upload some contacts. CLICK HERE to be taken to the contacts page of FronlineCloud. Note, this will take you away from this page to complete the import process.
  5. Get Support (optional)
    • You can always access our database of support documents here at support.frontlinesms.com. If you need to get ahold of someone from the team, you can get help here (LINK)

Radio Templates

Included in your FrontlineCloud workspace are some specialized Activities designed specifically for Radio users. These Activities are easy to configure like our classic Activities, but are tailored for the workflows for use with Radio.

Contest - Want to run a contest using a keyword? Use this Activity and announce to your users the first person to text ‘Text2Win’ will win a prize. This Activity lets you specify a keyword and an auto reply which will be sent to each contestant. The winner will be displayed in the Inbox with a keyword filter.

Send a Survey - You can publish a survey to all your subscribers at the push of a button. This survey will send a question and series of answers to a specified group or list of users. Users can reply using keywords which will automatically tabulate and graph responses.

Feedback - One of the most important uses for SMS alongside radio is to keep the conversation going. Using this Activity lets users text in responses
