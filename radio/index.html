Included in your FrontlineCloud workspace are some specialized Activities designed specifically for Radio users. These Activities are easy to configure like our classic Activities, but are tailored for the workflows for use with Radio.

Run a Contest - Want to run a contest using a keyword? Use this Activity and announce to your users the first person to text ‘Text2Win’ will win a prize. This Activity lets you specify a keyword and an auto reply which will be sent to each contestant. The winner will be displayed in the Inbox with a keyword filter.

Send a Survey - You can publish a survey to all your subscribers at the push of a button. This survey will send a question and series of answers to a specified group or list of users. Users can reply using keywords which will automatically tabulate and graph responses.

Request a Song - Use this Activity to let your audience select a song they would like to hear on your station. Provide a keyword, followed by a song title and this will show up in the inbox so that you can queue up which requests should be played next.

Frontline helps empower radio stations to communicate directly to listeners over SMS. Our new Activities let you launch polls to your listeners, allows listeners to request songs, take feedback and for users to register who they are.

By adding Frontline’s two way messaging to traditional media channels like radio, you can reach a whole new level of engagement and action as part of your existing outreach strategy.
